Projects

Okta Personal

Okta Personal is Okta's consumer password manager. It is available in the App Store.

Uno

Uno was an a16z-backed, user-friendly, ultra-secure password manager. The iOS, iPadOS and macOS apps were built using a single SwiftUI codebase. Uno was eventually acquired by Okta.

Aetna Health

Aetna Health is Aetna's flagship app.

iTriage Essentials

iTriage Essentials was a ground-up rewrite of iTriage. It was Aetna's first foray into Swift, but it was eventually retired in favor of Aetna Health.

iTriage

iTriage was a symptom checker and care locator created by ER doctors to help guide patients to the proper in-network level of care.

G-licious

G-licious was a quick and easy way to check the Dining Menu in the Grinnell College Dining Hall. G-licious provided nutritional information and filters based on dietary preferences. G-licious was fully supported on iOS, Android, and Windows Phone and was optimized for both phone and tablet layouts.

FaceTag

FaceTag was a game for Android and iOS, written for MHacks Winter 2014. Set up a game with your friends and specify the duration of the game (time or points). Everyone will be given a target to tag! The first person to get a picture of their target's face gets a point and the game proceeds to the next round. Game Rules: Find your target and acquire a picture with their face clearly visible. Picture cannot be a picture of a picture. Other hunters will confirm that the picture is an original picture.

KDIC

The KDIC Streaming App was a convenient way for anyone to tune into the KDIC radio station, based out of Grinnell College, IA. The app allowed you to pause and play the live stream and listen to podcasts. It also provided the user with content from the KDIC website, such as the program schedule.

Scarlet & Black (S&B)

S&B was a nicely formatted mobile news reader for Grinnell College's Scarlet & Black newspaper. S&B was fully supported on iOS and Android, and was optimized for both phone and tablet layouts.

DB

DB was the mobile version of Grinnell College's campus directory. DB was fully supported on iOS, optimized for both phone and tablet layouts.
