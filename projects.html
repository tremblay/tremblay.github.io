Projects

G-licious

G-licious is a quick and easy way to check the Dining Menu in the Grinnell Dining Hall. You can check the menu for today, tomorrow, or any day in the next week. G-licious also provides nutritional information and filters based on dietary preferences. G-licious is fully supported on iOS, Android, and Windows Phone and is optimized for both phone and tablet layouts.

Website: apps.g-licio.us

FaceTag

FaceTag is a fun game for Android and iOS, written for MHacks Winter 2014. Set up a game with your friends and specify the duration of the game (time or points). Everyone will be given a target to tag! The first person to get a picture of their target's face gets a point and the game proceeds to the next round. Game Rules: Find your target and acquire a picture with their face clearly visible. Picture cannot be a picture of a picture. Other hunters will confirm that the picture is an original picture.

Website: g-licio.us/FaceTag

KDIC

The KDIC Streaming App is a convenient way for anyone to tune into the KDIC radio station, based out of Grinnell College, IA. The app allows you to pause and play the live stream and listen to podcasts. It also brings the user content from the KDIC website, such as the program schedule.

Website: g-licio.us/KDIC/

Scarlet & Black (S&B)

S&B is a nicely formatted mobile news reader for Grinnell College's Scarlet & Black newspaper. S&B is fully supported on iOS and Android, and is optimized for both phone and tablet layouts.

Website: g-licio.us/SandB/

DB

DB is the mobile version of Grinnell College's campus directory. DB is fully supported on iOS and is coming soon on Android. It is optimized for both phone and tablet layouts.

Website: g-licio.us/DB/
